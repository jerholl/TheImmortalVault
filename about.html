// The Creator

Writer. Director.
Architect of Dread.

For as long as I can remember (scream 1997) I have been a horror fan. I've had my ups and downs with the genre over the years but I always crawl back. I enjoy horror and a handful of other genres in many different formats. That is novels, audiobooks, audiodramas, games, and more. I'll dabble in scifi and fantasy here and there but there's just something about horror that keeps me around longer. It's not necessarily the spooky feeling or wanting to be scared but the storytelling of it. The legends that have been created in the genre. The opportunities the genre affords to people especially to those who are starting out. It's a forgiving genre and a genre that even the greatest of the greats find their way back to. It's an important genre in the grand scheme of things. Yeah sometimes things go off the walls with gore and blood but sometimes there's also just a damn good story in there.

"What's your favorite scary movie?

Wes Craven is one of my favorite directors of all time. He introduced me to horror and I'll be honest, while there are a lot of great horror films, nothing has topped Scream to me but that's probably because I watched it such a young age the nostalgia is just too strong. Well anyway, enough rambling. Thanks for coming to my website!
